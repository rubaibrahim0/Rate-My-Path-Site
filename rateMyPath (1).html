Welcome to RateMyPath

Transform your campus experience like never before with RateMyPath, a platform designed to assist the disabled community in navigating the University of Maryland, College Park. Our mission is to ensure that every student is provided with the necessary knowledge to explore pathways that promote reliability and inclusion.

Navigating a campus as large as ours is an obstacle in itself, but the recent influx of construction has only compounded this challenge. Many disabled students describe getting around campus as anxiety-inducing, wishing that accessibility was more of a default response rather than an afterthought. We built RateMyPath to address this crucial need, foster community engagement around the urgency of inclusion, and serve as a supportive network.

By offering UMD a community-driven resource in which students can review and rate their path experiences based on accessibility and disability-friendliness, we strive to create a hub of shared feedback where students can utilize their peers' insights to move more confidently and freely around campus.
